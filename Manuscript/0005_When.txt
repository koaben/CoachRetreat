##When
A coachRetreat is held on Saturdays. This is one among a few numbers of string rules of a CoachRetreat. The spirit of a CoachRetreat is to create an inspirational and caring event for the community, while having huge fun. CoachRetreat is ment to be like a hobby. As you may spend your Saturday to go biking, hiking of play tennin with your frends, or local association, if may spend a Pleasent Saturday with your friends from the (Agile) Coaching community.
